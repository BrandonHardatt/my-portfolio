About Me

I'm a Full Stack Software Developer and Machine Learning Engineer with over three years of industry experience, holding a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Memorial University of Newfoundland. Raised in Wabush, NL, I’ve always cherished the unique natural beauty and community spirit of this province. My professional focus has centered on building scalable web and mobile apps, integrating machine learning solutions, and improving user experiences.

Throughout my career, I've collaborated with local entrepreneurs and larger organizations alike—ranging from creating React Native solutions for local tour companies to implementing real-time communication features with WebRTC. I’ve led a team at Health and Rehab Inc. to develop an AI-powered rehab app, making therapy more accessible. When I’m not coding, I volunteer my IT skills to local nonprofits, refurbishing donated computers to support families in need.

I love problem-solving, exploring new technologies, and contributing to projects that have a real-world impact. My motivation stems from a passion for continuous learning and a commitment to building solutions that empower people in my local community and beyond.
